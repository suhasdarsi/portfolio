Networks feel resilient when every node can reach another through only a few steps. That efficiency is usually created by a small number of highly connected hubs.

The same structure also concentrates failure. Remove an ordinary node and little changes. Compromise a hub and the shortest paths through the network become attack paths.

AI is becoming this kind of hub for organizational knowledge and action. Its value comes from connecting systems that used to remain separate.

The better it becomes at reducing distance, the more carefully we must examine what depends on it.
